About Us

The SM Store

The SM Store is the Philippines’ largest department store chain with 65 branches nationwide—with a great selection of menswear, womenswear, childrenswear, all kinds of beauty products, and things for the home. You can also find stationery, electronic gadgets, snacks, toys, hardware, and fun Philippine souvenirs here.

Beyond merchandise, The SM Store provides services such as bills payment, ticketing, remittance, currency exchange, free gift wrapping, clothing alterations, home delivery, and most recently, click & collect.

Since our first store opened in 1958, The SM Store has been a long-time favorite one-stop-shop with the best product variety, shopping convenience, and value for money.

The SM Store carries this ease of shopping online with www.thesmstore.com, combining the benefits of browsing online and shopping in-store. Stay up to date with the latest in fashion and be the first to see what’s new in our stores via TheSMStore.com.

As we always say, "We've got it all for you!"
