What's New SMART Free Load

Promo

SMART Free
Load Promo

January 21, 2020

3.6k SHARES Share Tweet Copy Link

Buy Smart Load cards worth P300 and P500 at The SM Store near you, and get free P100 call card in return!

Promo Mechanics

  1. This promo is open to all Smart prepaid customers.
  2. Promo is valid from October 9 to November 15, 2020 at The SM Store branches nationwide.
  3. Customers who buy two (2) P300 Smart, Sun, or TNT call cards will get one free load card worth P100.
  4. Customers who buy two (2) P500 Smart, Sun, or TNT call cards will get two free load cards worth P100 each.
  5. Customers who buy two (2) P500 Smart, Sun, or TNT call cards will get two free load cards worth P100 each.
  6. The free load card will be released by The SM Store counter personnel to the customer upon payment.
  7. Customers are only allowed to buy a maximum of two (2) sets of cards (4 cards in total) per transaction.
  8. Customers can only redeem the free load card/s once per day.
  9. Customers may only pay with cash to purchase call cards.
  10. The free load card cannot be converted to cash or discount, and cannot be exchanged for any other item.

This promo is available in all The SM Store branches nationwide from October 9 to November 15, 2020. Per DTI Fair Trade Permit No.
